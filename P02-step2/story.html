Our brand History

In 2017, we were born in Vancouver. Modern people's life is getting faster and faster, and our founder Susan, after graduating from college, calms down to think about the health and value that nature can bring to humans. Using her expertise in nutrition at the university, Susan created this product and quickly gained a foothold in the industry with high quality products.

Our brand Purpose

Our goal is to promote this healthy lifestyle to more places and allow more people to receive and enjoy this healthy lifestyle. Provide customers with the best shopping experience at a reasonable price and exquisite service. Let the public know that this healthy and high-quality life does not require high prices, and will not increase the economic burden of the original life, but will gain a healthier body and more energy to experience the beauty of life.
